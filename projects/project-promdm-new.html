Project Highlights:

The main goal of UI redesign was to reduce visual clutter of existing UI, but at the same time keep the same sequence of steps and grouping of information the users got used to.
That required thorough layout redesign - moving top navigation to the left, introducing 2-column main content layout and a sidebar.
The next step was to prioritise the contents and actions - place crucial ones in more accessible areas of the UI, move less important ones out of the way. New layout also enabled more efficient tracking and comparing data, which appeared to be one of the more important tasks and needed a better support.
Simple and clean design reduces visual clutter and highlights most important contents and actions.
