Who I Am

Hi, I’m Mirna, a senior product designer with over a decade of experience in digital design.

I strive to create meaningful and intuitive user experiences that solve real-world problems while aligning with business goals.

My Approach to Design

I focus on simplicity and productivity, blending product strategy, design and development to ensure that the product is visually appealing, works impeccably and meets business requirements.

Iterative approach to design and implementation ensures that every feature is meaningful, user friendly and works as planned.

My Journey

Professional Background

Throughout my career I’ve worked in several different industries (education, administration, sales & marketing), but one thing I’ve always loved is solving complex problems with simple design solutions. In 2010 I decided to pursue a career in web design and development, and later on specialized in UX/UI and product design.

Personal Projects

I’m always looking for ways to improve the world around me through design. One such example is Farmicon, a tool I developed to help small local farmers connect with their regular customers, offering an intuitive platform for weekly offers, orders, and deliveries.
